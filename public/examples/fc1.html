Fog Creek Software (formerly known as PaxDigita) solves web content management problems with our world-class software and expert consulting practice.

Today's web site management tools are too primitive and too painful. Almost everyone who works with a web site that changes frequently is feeling this pain.

Fog Creek takes a two-pronged approach to fixing content management problems. First, we are continuously developing and enhancing a set of high performance, easy to use software tools for content management. Second, since every web site has its own specific requirements, our SWAT team of programming gurus tailors the software for your installation.

We only hire the best software engineers in the world. To attract them, we're trying to create the hands-down best work environment for computer professionals anywhere in the world. Making Fog Creek Software a great place to work is priority number one and we are passionate about it.

Fog Creek is not just about working on the coolest products: it's about the journey being the reward. At Fog Creek, it will be fun to come to work in the morning. Fog Creek offers highly competitive compensation, stock options, free lunch, full benefits and six weeks of paid vacation. We plan to provide private offices with doors and a professional environment where you have an opportunity to work on something exciting with the smartest minds in the field.

When you consider that you spend almost all of your daylight hours at work, the typical modus operandi of working in dark, noisy cubicle farms for micromanager jerks with unsocial coworkers in the hopes of some "big payoff" in the future just doesn't cut it. It is not sustainable. The workplace is a community. The very quality of our lives depends on creating workplace communities that nourish and sustain us. And that's what Fog Creek is all about.
